Application Requirements

To apply for the Power Learn Project, you must meet the following criteria:

  1. Be between 18 and 35 years of age
  2. Commit to completing the full program duration (6 months)
  3. Have access to a computer and stable internet connection
  4. Demonstrate passion for technology and learning
  5. Be available for at least 20 hours per week for the program
